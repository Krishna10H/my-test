The Weave

Guide two linked orbs through the weave. Move, stretch the rope, dodge obstacles, and collect coins as the track speeds up.

Skins & Achievements

Unlock new orb skins by earning achievements as you play - collect coins, chain combos, thread near-misses, and clear gauntlets.

How to Play

↔️
Move — Use ← → / ◀ ▶ or the left on-screen D-pad to shift the rope left and right.
↕️
Stretch the rope — Use ↑ ↓ / ▲ ▼ or the right D-pad to widen or narrow the gap between the two orbs.
🧵
Objective — Weave upward as far as you can. Only the two orbs are dangerous — the rope between them safely crosses over obstacles.
⚠️
Avoid — Walls, gates, spinning bars, and moving hazards. Some need a narrow rope, others need it wide enough to straddle a pillar.
🛡️
Power-ups — Grab the glowing blue orb for a few seconds of shield that lets you ride straight through anything.
🏆
Progress — The track levels up and speeds up the further you go. Collect coins along the way for bonus score.
